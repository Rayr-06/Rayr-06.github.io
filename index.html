// 05 · How I Think

My Approach

Most software testing focuses only on identifying defects after they appear.

My approach is different. I believe testing should evolve into intelligent systems that help developers build more reliable software from the start — not just catch problems, but prevent them.

By combining quality assurance experience with automation thinking and AI-driven tools, my goal is to move testing from a manual, reactive process into a predictive and intelligent engineering discipline.

The same philosophy drives RAYR: instead of reacting to market moves, give traders a systematic edge — the same kind of edge that Jim Simons used to build the most profitable trading fund in history.
